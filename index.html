About

Hi, I’m Micah Muñoz, a Computer Science, Spanish, and Theology student at Benedictine College. When I'm not running or in school, I spend time building the faith community on campus and leading men's groups. I'm especially interested in expanding my skillset with software projects, learning about cloud services, and developing my programming skills. On a cool evening, you’ll find me hanging with friends, having a campfire, and enjoying creation. Reach out and let's connect!
